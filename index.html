Pure Shenandoah Paek

We are a highly sustainable, private outdoor park, located in Arlingotn, VA

Much of the material is made from our locally grown hemp

We are committed to providing our members with a healthy, green, and affordable recreational location

What We Have To Offer

Restaurant: A quick, heart healthy farm to table menu with tables and chairs for members to sit and eat.

Courts: Sustainable hardwood hemp flooring with extra bright solar powered LED lights.

Stage: For concert venues with hemp flooring, speakers, and party lights.

Playground: Hemp based high density polyethylene and hemp mulch for children to play on.

Pavillion avialable for rent (free for Gold tier members).

Sporting equipment available for rent (free for Gold tier members).
